Privacy Policy for MB LEGACY STORE

Last Updated: 1 May 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to MB LEGACY STORE, a platform powered by Pi Network. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application at https://mblegacystore.vercel.app.

2. Information We Collect

We practice minimal data collection:

  • Pi Username: Shared via Pi SDK for authentication.
  • Transaction Data: Payment IDs and metadata.

We DO NOT collect or store:

  • Wallet passphrases or private keys.
  • Phone numbers, addresses, or ID documents.

3. How We Use Information

  • To authenticate your identity.
  • To process and complete payment transactions.
  • To display your transaction history.

4. Data Sharing

We DO NOT sell, trade, or rent your personal data. Transaction data on the Pi blockchain is public by nature.

5. Data Security

Access is managed through encrypted API keys. We never store your private keys.

6. Pi Network Compliance

This app complies with Pi Network's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy.

7. Your Rights

You may disconnect your wallet anytime. Contact us for data-related requests.
